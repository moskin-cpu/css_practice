Welcome to **My Demo Site**

This is the main content area. This site is designed to demonstrate various CSS techniques, including **float** layouts, **absolute** and **relative** positioning, **background images**, and the use of the **box-shadow** property. We are using **Devonshire** font for the header, which is loaded from Google Fonts.

The navigation bar uses a clever **centering technique** that involves floating the `ul` left and then using a combination of `left: 50%` on the `ul` and `right: 50%` on the `li` elements.
